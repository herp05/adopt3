Our Mission

Our goal is for our target audience to realize that it’s better to adopt than shop. We want the audience to go to adoption centers instead of stores and save animals that need a home.

The overall message we are trying to convey to our target audience is to adopt, don’t shop. This is an important message because when you adopt you help an animal in need. Most animals that are in shelters are either lost, left out in the streets, or were treated terribly by their previous owner.

It’s also important because you save a life, you pay less, and help stop cruelty in mass breeding facilities. The methods we will use to convey the overall message are by creating a poster, website, and totes/stickers and spreading the message. We could also encourage people to adopt by setting the example and adopting an animal or showing examples of people who have adopted pets.
